Stargazing With You

A poem by Marthea Lourdes G. Pebojot

A clear night sky is a beautiful wonder

For we can see all the stars hover

That night was the first time I saw the most stunning arrangement of lights

I thought, "what else could be prettier than this sight?"

I saw Mars, Venus, and the Big Dipper

Only some of the brightest sights I saw that night

At that moment, I knew I could never be happier

To be able so see the stars with you under the moonlight

That evening we spent stargazing

Was a moment I could never think of forgetting

The feeling of the cold breeze under a fascinating sky

Made it feel more real, even while seeing it with my own eyes

That day itself was eventful

Starting from the moment they gave out hot cocoa

When we listened to music right after the event was over,

I listened to the rain and felt carefree and peaceful

We've been friends for years, and I remembered every moment I treasured

From when we first met to the happy moments we shared together

Yet no other memory could compare to this

Stargazing with you was definitely a moment of bliss.
